Oriole Community Centre

The newly renovated Oriole Community Centre is a fully accessible facility with the following features: a full-size gymnasium, four multi-purpose rooms, preschool room, teaching kitchen, weight room, dance studio. Oriole CC is located at 2975 Don Mills Road West just north of Fairview Mall. Hours of operation are 7 am to 11 pm daily, seven days a week, excluding all statutory holidays.
